Hello, My name is Zach!
Welcome to my personal blog.

I was born and raised in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia for the first 19 years of my life, before coming to the United States in August, 2019 to pursue my Bachelor's of Science in physics.

I'm currently an academic researcher looking to transition into data science.

Broadly speaking, I’m interested in tackling questions that involve large datasets. My work so far within observational astronomy and SETI draws heavily from a combination of deep learning and statistical inference techniques, in an attempt to shed light on these fundamental yet profound questions.

More recently, I’ve enjoyed developing novel machine learning methods commonly employed in NLP, alongside applying standard statistical methods, to maximally harness information from these data.
